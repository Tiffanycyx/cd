How To Make French Toast


Description

For this quick and easy French toast recipe you will need to start with some vanilla extract to create a rich flavor. Pick your favorite bread - white, Italian, French, or whole wheat. Learn how to make quick and easy French toast today!


Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 4 slicesz of bread
  • pancake syrup
  • strawberries
  • blueberries

Steps

  1. Step One: Beat egg, vanilla extract in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk.
  2. Step Two: Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Step Three: Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides.
  4. Step Four: Pour pancake syrup if desired, serve warm with fruits. Voila! Easy french toast!
